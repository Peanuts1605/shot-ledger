## Title

Shot Ledger: The Decision Receipt for AI-Generated Media

## Description

An approved AI shot often arrives as a lonely file. Shot Ledger keeps the
keeper, the takes it beat, the exact generation recipe, and the human reason it
won together as one verifiable decision packet.

In this demo, Genblaze creates three controlled takes while changing one named
creative variable. Backblaze B2 stores every image, provenance manifest,
partial-run checkpoint, and the final human decision. A fresh process reloads
the complete packet from B2 and verifies its hashes before the public review
surface serves it read-only.

Shot Ledger does not ask a model to choose the winner. Generation supplies the
candidates and machine provenance. A human supplies the creative judgment.
